Characters

Akari Itsuya - The Detective

Role: Protagonist

A brilliant detective with exceptional deductive abilities and sharp observational skills. Akari can piece together complex scenarios from minimal clues—from determining accident details at a glance to analyzing evidence in his home lab. He combines intellect with unexpected physical prowess (catching punches mid-air) and has a calm, methodical approach to even the most bizarre cases. Despite being a smoker who acknowledges its harm, he uses it as part of his investigative ritual. Akari's defining trait is his relentless pursuit of truth, no matter how impossible it seems. When he remembers the glitches, he experiences intense headaches and bleeding, but his determination never wavers. He reads dark-themed books like Haruki Murakami's "Hard-Boiled Wonderland and the End of the World."

Hikari - The Detective's Wife

Role: Supporting Character / Partner

Akari's wife, who serves as both his emotional support and investigative partner. Hikari is observant and caring, noticing details like the cigarettes on the floor that indicate Akari couldn't solve a case. She scolds him about smoking and his habit of mumbling while walking. Despite her playful complaints, she's deeply committed to helping Akari, even in the face of terrifying supernatural phenomena. She recently returned from visiting her parents. Hikari's presence grounds Akari and helps him piece together fragmented memories.

Kaito - The Disappeared Friend

Role: Supporting Character (Missing)

Akari's friend, who attended the party and was supposed to stay overnight due to the rain. Kaito is the catalyst for the entire mystery—his disappearance (or "glitching") from reality sets Akari on a path to uncover the truth. Even his own mother claims she never had a son, suggesting the glitches erase not just the person but all memories and records of their existence. His house is found in a mess, and his last call with his mother was 5 months ago.

Kenji Morita - The Witness Who Became a Victim

Role: Key Witness

A driver who first reported seeing someone "glitch out of existence" after hitting them with his car. By the next day, Kenji himself began forgetting the incident, experiencing headaches when trying to remember. At exactly midnight during police interrogation, Kenji glitched out of existence while saying "I remember heading ho—" He became a victim of the very phenomenon he witnessed. After his disappearance, everyone forgot he existed, proving the pattern: people glitch at midnight and are erased from all memories.

Kei Kagami

Role: ???

Overview

Shinichi Kudo, a brilliant high school detective, is transformed into a child after being poisoned by a mysterious criminal organization.

Taking on the alias "Conan Edogawa," he continues to solve complex cases while living with his childhood friend Ran and her father, private detective Kogoro Mouri. All while searching for the organization that shrunk him and a way to return to his original form.

With his incredible deductive abilities and an arsenal of gadgets from Professor Agasa, Conan tackles murder mysteries, kidnappings, and criminal conspiracies—all while keeping his true identity a secret.

Main Characters

Shinichi Kudo / Conan Edogawa

A brilliant 17-year-old detective who is transformed into a child. As Conan, he continues solving cases while hiding his true identity and searching for a cure.

Ran Mouri

Shinichi's childhood friend and love interest. A karate champion who remains unaware of Conan's true identity while waiting for Shinichi's return.

Kogoro Mouri

Ran's father and a private detective. Often put to sleep by Conan to solve cases, earning him the nickname "Sleeping Kogoro."

Ai Haibara

A former member of the Black Organization who was also transformed into a child. She works with Conan to find an antidote.
